Upcoming Events

Kids Camp

Calling all young adventurers! Our annual Kids Camp is just around the corner, promising a week filled with laughter, friendship, and unforgettable experiences in nature. Let's ignite the spirit of curiosity and joy in our youngest members as they embark on this exciting journey together.

Prayer Meeting

Join us as we come together in unity and devotion during our weekly prayer meeting. Let's uplift one another in prayer, seeking guidance, solace, and blessings from the divine. Together, we can create a powerful atmosphere of faith and support.

Mens Conference

Calling all men of valor! Our Men's Conference is a time for fellowship, growth, and empowerment. Dive deep into discussions on faith, family, purpose, and masculinity, and emerge strengthened and inspired to lead with courage and compassion in every aspect of life.

Youth Convergence

Calling all young hearts and passionate souls! Our Youth Convergence is a gathering like no other, where inspiration meets action. Get ready for dynamic speakers, engaging workshops, and opportunities to connect with peers who share your vision for a brighter future.

Leadership Conference

Leaders aren't born; they're cultivated. Whether you're a seasoned leader or aspiring to step into leadership roles, our Leadership Conference is designed to equip you with the tools, insights, and strategies needed to lead with purpose, integrity, and compassion.

Baptism Sunday

A momentous occasion awaits as we celebrate the spiritual journeys of those ready to take the next step in their faith. Join us in rejoicing as individuals publicly proclaim their commitment to Christ through the sacred act of baptism. It's a day of renewal, grace, and profound significance for our church family.
