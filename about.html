About Us

Welcome to Evangelical Deliverance Ministry

Evangelistic Deliverance Ministries is a faith-based organization dedicated to spiritual deliverance and evangelism.Founded on Christian principles, these ministries focus on liberating individuals from spiritual oppression and leading them to a deeper relationship with God.

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the transformative love of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to bring hope, healing, and deliverance through the power of the Gospel. At Evangelical Deliverance Ministries, we believe in the life-changing power of prayer, the importance of biblical teaching, and the strength found in a supportive, faith-filled community.

Join us as we seek to make a difference in the lives of individuals and families, helping them find freedom and purpose in Christ. Whether you are searching for a spiritual home, in need of deliverance, or looking to grow in your faith, we welcome you with open arms. Together, let's experience the abundant life that God promises.
