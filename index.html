About Us

Welcome to The Evangelical Deliverance Ministry

Evangelistic Deliverance Ministries is a faith-based organization dedicated to spiritual deliverance and evangelism.

Founded on Christian principles, these ministries focus on liberating individuals from spiritual oppression and leading them to a deeper relationship with God.
